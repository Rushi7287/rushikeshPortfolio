30.c Error Handling Strategy

üé§INTERVIEW SECTION
30-Second Interview Answer

An effective error handling strategy in Salesforce involves capturing exceptions, logging them in a

structured way, preventing full transaction failures where appropriate, implementing retry mechanisms

for async processes, and ensuring user-friendly error messaging without exposing sensitive system

  • details.
