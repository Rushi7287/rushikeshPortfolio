19.c Middleware Integration

30-Second Interview Answer
Middleware Integration is an architectural pattern where a middleware platform (like MuleSoft) acts as

an intermediary between Salesforce and other systems. It handles transformation, orchestration,

security, monitoring, and retry logic, enabling scalable and loosely coupled enterprise integrations.
