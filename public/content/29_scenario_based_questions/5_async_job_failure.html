30-Second Interview Answer

Async Job Failure occurs when asynchronous processes like Future, Queueable, Batch, or Scheduled

Apex fail due to unhandled exceptions, governor limits, callout errors, or data issues. Proper error

handling, logging, monitoring via AsyncApexJob, and retry mechanisms are essential to manage failures.

  • Monitoring tools:
‚úî AsyncApexJob object

‚úî Email notifications
