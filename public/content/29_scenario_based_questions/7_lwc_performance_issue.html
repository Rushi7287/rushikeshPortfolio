30-Second Interview Answer

LWC performance issues usually occur due to excessive Apex calls, non-selective queries, improper

reactivity handling, unnecessary re-rendering, large data loads, or lack of caching. Optimization involves

using @wire efficiently, enabling caching, minimizing DOM updates, and ensuring backend queries are

  • selective.
