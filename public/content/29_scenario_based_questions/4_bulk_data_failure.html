29.d Bulk Data Failure

30-Second Interview Answer
Bulk data failure occurs when processing large volumes of records (via Data Loader, Batch Apex, or API)

and the transaction fails due to validation errors, governor limits, non-selective queries, or unhandled

exceptions. Proper bulkification, partial DML, and error logging strategies are required to prevent

  • complete transaction rollback.
