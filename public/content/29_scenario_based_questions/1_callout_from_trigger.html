29.a Callout from Trigger

30-Second Interview Answer

Salesforce does not allow synchronous HTTP callouts directly from triggers because triggers execute

within database transactions. To perform a callout from a trigger, we must use asynchronous Apex like

@future(callout=true), Queueable Apex, or Platform Events.
