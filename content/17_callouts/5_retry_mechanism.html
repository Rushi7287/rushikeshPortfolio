30-Second Interview Answer

A Retry Mechanism in Salesforce integration is a strategy to reattempt failed callouts or external

operations in a controlled manner. It is typically implemented using asynchronous Apex like Queueable

or Scheduled Apex, combined with error logging and exponential backoff to avoid system overload.
