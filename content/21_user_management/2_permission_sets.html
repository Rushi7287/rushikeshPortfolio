30-Second Interview Answer

Permission Sets in Salesforce are used to grant additional permissions to users without modifying their

profile. Unlike profiles, a user can have multiple permission sets, making access management more

  • flexible and scalable.

Permission Set = Additional access (optional, multiple per user)
