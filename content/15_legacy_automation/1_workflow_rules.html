30-Second Interview Answer

Workflow Rules are legacy Salesforce automation tools used to automate simple business processes like

field updates, email alerts, task creation, and outbound messages when record criteria are met.

Salesforce has deprecated Workflow Rules in favor of Flow, but they still exist in many legacy orgs.
