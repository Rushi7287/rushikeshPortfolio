30-Second Interview Answer

CRUD & FLS enforcement in Apex ensures that users can only create, read, update, or delete records and

fields they have permission for. Since Apex runs in system mode, developers must explicitly check

object-level (CRUD) and field-level (FLS) permissions to prevent unauthorized access.
