30-Second Interview Answer

Trigger and Flow interaction refers to how Apex triggers and Salesforce Flows execute within the same

transaction during the record save process. Since both can update records, they can cause re-entry,

recursion, and unexpected field overwrites if not designed carefully. Understanding their execution

  • order is critical to avoid conflicts and CPU limit issues.

Before-Save Flow

  • Option 2: Apex-First Strategy

  • Large data volume
  • Integration
  • Performance critical

When NOT to Mix
  • Avoid mixing Flow + Trigger if:

  • Same business logic executed
  • Large data volume environment
  • High integration traffic
